Billing FAQ
When does my trial end?

Your 14-day free trial starts when you create your account. You will not be charged until the trial ends. You can cancel anytime before the trial ends with no charge.

Can I switch plans?

Yes! You can upgrade or downgrade at any time. Upgrades take effect immediately. Downgrades take effect at the next billing cycle.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit and debit cards (Visa, Mastercard, Amex, Discover) via Stripe. All payments are securely processed — PropFlow never stores your card details.

How do I cancel?

Click "Manage subscription" above to access the Stripe billing portal where you can cancel at any time. You'll retain access until the end of your current billing period.
